Guest Parking

Parking is free and available onsite. The venue has a large parking lot that accommodates more than 7,000 vehicles. Plan to arrive early because traffic, security checks and the walk to the venue entrance can take time.

Amphitheater parking guide

Rehearsal

Rehearsal will be held from 3:45 to 4:30 p.m. Friday, May 2 at Cascades Amphitheater. Attendance is optional but recommended.

Attend the rehearsal to:

  • Get familiar with the venue.
  • Practice processing in and out.
  • Meet your name reader and practice pronunciation of your name.
  • Pick up your name card and honor cord (if applicable).

Graduates should arrive by 3:15 p.m. to check in for rehearsal. Free parking will be available at the venue beginning at 2:45 p.m.

Commencement Day Schedule

The ceremony will take place at 1 p.m. Saturday, May 3 at Cascades Amphitheater

  • Noon – Graduates arrive at the amphitheater.
  • 12:15 p.m. – Graduates must be in the gathering area to line up.
  • 12:45 p.m. – Processional begins. We’re not waiting for you!
  • 1 p.m. – Ceremony begins.
  • 3 p.m. – Ceremony ends.

Graduates will cross the stage as their names are read and will receive a diploma cover.

Helpful Tips for a Smooth Commencement Experience

  • Plan ahead: Arrive early to allow time for parking, security screening and walking to your gathering area.
  • Wear comfortable shoes: The venue includes gravel, pavement, stairs and ramps. Choose footwear that is comfortable for walking and standing.
  • Keep personal items to a minimum: There is no storage for bags or belongings. Small clear bags or clutches are allowed, but everything you bring in must stay with you throughout the entire ceremony. If you can, leave personal items with family or friends during the ceremony.
  • Listen to the marshals: They are there to guide you through the ceremony. Following their instructions will ensure the event runs smoothly.
  • Establish a meet-up plan: Graduates and guests will exit through different areas. Decide ahead of time where to reunite after the ceremony.
  • Use the official commencement hashtags: Share your photos and celebrate your achievement using #WSUV2025 and #CougGrad on social media.

WSU Vancouver Commencement volunteers will be available to help you the day of the ceremony.
